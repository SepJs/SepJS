## 👾 About Me

🎮 I'm **Unknown XRG** (Sepanta) — a multidisciplinary creator who lives at the intersection of **code**, **art**, and **game design**.

🌌 Founder and sole creator of **INNER VOID** — a game experience forged from nothing but vision, logic, and obsession with craft.

🎨 I work across the full creative stack: from **low-level C** to **3D sculpting in ZBrush**, from **pixel art in Aseprite** to **composing music in MuseScore**.

🔐 I have a strong foundation in **cybersecurity** — because building things means knowing how to protect them.

📚 Advancing academically through **Harvard Online** — certificate on the way.
